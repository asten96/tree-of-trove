FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

How do I use this?

So to start using our application, simply type in any search term and wait a few seconds. Shortly, the rings should start to form properly and the chart will take form. Again, shortly after that, bubbles will appear on the outside of the ring which will house pictures taken from the search you just did. That is essentially it! The application is fully interactive and you can click on rings to view them better, and mouse over images to enlarge them.

What is the Trove of Life?

The Trove of Life is a data visualisation tool for the Trove database. From a single search term, the application retrieves the search results and applies them to multiple pie charts, which will load after a few seconds. What you can see in the application is that each ring is a category of search, for example image or article, and the pie chart part represents that data in the relevant subcategory.

Why Make the Trove of Life?

Data is the big word of today. So many companies revolve around the storing of large amounts of data and such, and is always the talk in the IT industry. But what if you wanted to visualise that data within a certain search? Well, that’s where the idea of the Trove of Life comes in. While this particularly applies to the Trove database, this application can be applied to many different searches with a great API such as Trove’s.

Can I access FAQ within the application?

Of course, all you have to do is click the information button which will either be placed top left or the bottom left of your screen. This will bring up these exact FAQ for you to read.

What features are part of the application?

This application simply represents the data within a Trove search. As such, it has been made so that access to most of that is readily available. Within the circle itself, you can mouseover the respective parts of the pie chart and look at how many results were pulled from said search. If you want a larger look at a specific section, simply click on that section and it will enlarge for you.

The pictures that come with the search (provided there are images available) will pop up around the circle. These can be moused over to have a closer look at. The leaves that are in the application are simply there to create the tree feel, and also shows that a new search is made by it's animation.
There is also a legend on the left hand side that shows the sections are the color of said section.
